Our community

LOGO provides the best platform for Startups as well as Mentors. We come across various innovative and mind boggling ideas and also the people who are interested in guiding them.

Every idea and plan is mapped with there respective specialized mentor. We provide mentors from all over the Globe with their best tips.

Our mission

To provide the best possible guidance and experienced mentor to make the journey fruitful. There are enormous number of ideas but inly few achieve success, we aim to make this few count.

There are many perks of a successful startup. Making a single startup successful gives not only the owning team benefit but also is a source of job opportunity. Even the mentors get to earn profit.
